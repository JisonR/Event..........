Event Overview:

PitchPerfect 2024 is an intercollege competition that brings together bright minds to showcase their
entrepreneurial skills and innovative ideas. Our goal is to foster creativity, collaboration, and a spirit
of innovation among students.

Objectives:

- Encourage innovative thinking and problem-solving
- Provide a platform for students to present their ideas
- Offer valuable feedback from industry experts
- Promote networking and collaboration among participants

Rounds Explanation:

1. Round 1 - Initial Presentation:
- Participants will present their business ideas, which must include SWOT
analysis, root cause analysis, marketing strategies, and competitor analysis. Additional content is at the
participants' discretion.

2. Round 2 - Prototype/Design Presentation:
- Participants will present a basic prototype or design. For hardware projects, a design is sufficient.

3. Round 3 - Final Presentation:
- Finalists will present their detailed business plans to industry experts, incorporating feedback from
the previous rounds.

Event Schedule:

9:00 AM - 9:30 AM:

Registration
• Participants check-in and receive welcome kits.

9:30 AM - 10:00 AM:

Inauguration Ceremony
• Welcome address by the Head of Department (HOD).
• Overview of the event.

10:00 AM - 10:45 AM:

Preparation for Round 1
• Participants prepare for their initial presentations.

10:45 AM - 11:15 AM:

Round 1 - Initial Presentations
• Each team delivers a 5-minute presentation.
• Judges provide 2 minutes of feedback per team.

11:15 AM - 12:15 PM:

Preparation for Round 2
• Participants prepare for the Rapid Prototyping round.

12:15 PM - 1:00 PM:

Round 2 - Rapid Prototyping
• Teams present their rapid prototypes in 5-minute sessions.
• Presentations are judged on innovation and feasibility.

1:00 PM - 1:30 PM:

Lunch Break
1:30 PM - 2:30 PM:

Preparation for Round 3
• Participants prepare for their Pitch Deck presentations.

2:30 PM - 3:30 PM:

Round 3 - Pitch Deck Presentation
• Teams present their pitch decks in 10-minute sessions.
• Each presentation is followed by a 5-minute Q&A session with the judges.

3:30 PM - 4:30 PM:

Award Ceremony and Closing Remarks • Announcement of winners.
• Distribution of awards.
• Closing remarks and group photo session.
